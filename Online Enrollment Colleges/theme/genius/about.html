About

Welcome to Makati Science Technological Institute

BRIEF HISTORY

Known before as MAKATI SCIENCE TECHNOLOGICAL INSTITUTE (MSTI), an educational institution established in Makati City, under the management of well-experienced people, sharing common vision of providing skills and technology education in a manner different from the other private technical schools.

MISSION

To make quality education accessible to all people regardless of social classes and financial capabilities providing them courses and disciplines comparative to those given in other universities and colleges.

VISION

A community of administrators, teachers, parents, and students working together to produce competent graduates and skilled workers whose individual lives are improved and thus making them productive members of the society.
